# AI Debugging Workflow

## Step 1

Collect OpenLane error log.

## Step 2

Provide relevant log section to AI.

## Step 3

Request:

* Root cause analysis
* Verification strategy
* Potential fixes

## Step 4

Validate suggestions manually.

## Step 5

Apply fix.

## Step 6

Re-run flow.

## Step 7

Compare results with previous execution.

## Outcome

Reduced debugging time and improved understanding of mixed-signal integration challenges.
